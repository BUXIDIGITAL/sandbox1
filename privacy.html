Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At ResumeBuilder, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our resume building service.

The short version: We don't collect or store your personal resume data. Everything stays in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Resume Data (Stored Locally)

When you use our resume builder, all of your resume information (name, contact details, work experience, education, etc.) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Remains under your complete control

1.2 Analytics Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our service, including:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • General location (country/city level only)

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

1.3 Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analytics (if you consent)
  • Essential site functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect your resume data, we can't use it. The anonymous analytics data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Understand how people use our tool
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your resume data is stored in your browser's localStorage, which means:

  • Data persists between sessions on the same device
  • Data is specific to your browser and device
  • Clearing your browser data will delete your resume
  • We recommend downloading your resume as a backup

3.2 Security Measures

While we don't store your data, we implement security best practices:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • No third-party data sharing

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

4.1 Analytics

We may use analytics services (like Google Analytics) to understand how our site is used. These services collect anonymous data only.

4.2 Hosting

Our website is hosted on Vercel. They may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, access times) for security and performance purposes.

4.3 CDN Services

We use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to serve JavaScript libraries (jsPDF, html2canvas) for PDF generation. These are loaded from public CDNs and don't collect personal data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in your browser's localStorage
  • Delete: Clear your resume data at any time by clearing browser storage
  • Export: Download your resume as a PDF at any time
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics cookies through your browser settings

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. International Users

Our service is available globally. Since your resume data stays in your browser, there are no international data transfers of your personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Posting a notice on our homepage
  • Sending an email notification (if we have your email)

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@resumebuilder.com
  • Website: ResumeBuilder.com

10. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • We process minimal personal data
  • Data is stored locally on your device
  • You have full control over your data
  • We don't share data with third parties
  • You can delete your data at any time

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
